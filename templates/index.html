About me

Fair warning: that picture is from years ago. I look radically different now.

On April 1, 2022, I wrote my first block of Python code; print('Hello World'). I haven't stopped coding since. Today, I build things that matter. Using Machine Learning and Deep Learning (mostly for Natural Language Processing tasks), I design products that solve real problems for real people. My current passion project is Kaawe, an open-source tool I'm developing to help children become better readers and thinkers. Why? Because every kid deserves the chance to fall in love with reading the way I did.

My goal in life is to democratize access to the things that make life worth living
